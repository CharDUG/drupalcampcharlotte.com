Hey there, welcome to the home of the inaugural DrupalCamp Charlotte. This camp is put on by the good folks at the Charlotte Drupal Users Group (CharDUG), a collection of enthusiastic individuals smitten with Drupal. Sunday, June 10, 2012 will feature a bevy of breakout sessions, birds of a feather (BoF) groups, and a keynote speech at the Blake Hotel in Charlotte's Uptown neighborhood. Our sessions and groups will have a little something for everyone, catering to everyone from the Dru-curious to seasoned API wonks.

Be sure to also register with the SouthEast LinuxFest website if you intend on joining us at DrupalCamp Charlotte. If you select participation in the DrupalCamp, you will be directed back to this site to complete the DrupalCamp portion of your registration. Registration is free for both the Camp and the Fest, but donations by way of selecting SELF's 2012 Supporter Package upon registration are always appreciated.

Remember to propose a session once you have registered.

See you there!
