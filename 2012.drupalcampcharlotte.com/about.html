About

DrupalCamp Charlotte. This camp is put on by the good folks at the Charlotte Drupal Users Group (CharDUG), a collection of enthusiastic individuals smitten with Drupal. Sunday, June 10, 2012 will feature a bevy of breakout sessions, birds of a feather (BoF) groups, and a keynote speech at the Blake Hotel in Charlotte's Uptown neighborhood. Our sessions and groups will have a little something for everyone, catering to everyone from the Dru-curious to seasoned API wonks.

Sponsorship opportunities for DrupalCamp Charlotte

What is DrupalCamp Charlotte?

DrupalCamp Charlotte is a soon-to-be-annual community event; this year being the inaugural. Our goal is to coalesce the wide array of southeastern Drupal developers, themers, designers, hackers, tinkerers and curious folk who've been hearing a lot about this Drupal stuff on the Internet. In short, we want 'em all! We want anyone who has a will to learn more, or anyone who knows more and has a will to share. It is the Drupal way. And the best way to appeal to to the widest array of interested attendees is to offer a high-quality experience at little to no cost to any and all comers. That is where our sponsors come in.

Why sponsor?

There are few among us who have not benefitted directly from the strength of the Drupal community. That strength comes from contributions at all levels. A contribution could be taking a few minutes to show a buddy this really neat thing you figured in Views. It could be writing a blog post about a problem you solved that may help others avoid it in the future. It is bug testing contrib modules. It is contributing to core. It is presenting at your local Drupal users group, or putting together a DrupalCamp. And it is sponsoring events like these!

By sponsoring DrupalCamp Charlotte, you are giving back to the community. Your sponsorship helps make it easier to bring in high-calibre speakers. It helps offset facilities costs, A/V equipment costs, and material costs. Sponsorship helps keep the cost to attendees as minimal as possible, which in turn brings in more. More attendees means more folks who will bring strength to the community. It also means more eyes to see your logo on the DCC site or proudly displayed on a sponsor table. Eyes to see that you or your company kick butt because you sponsored DrupalCamp Charlotte and helped make it all happen!
