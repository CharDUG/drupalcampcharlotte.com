Session Voting Now Open

Ladies and gentlemen, the window for session submissions has closed and we are ready for the next phase: voting! As of 8:00 AM, Monday May 14th, voting for DrupalCamp Charlotte 2012’s is open. To take part in the process, head over to the Sessions page and review the list of accepted sessions. If you see a proposal for a session you would like to attend, click on the title to go to the full node. At the bottom of the node is a “Vote for this session” link. Click on it and your vote is cast! You can rinse and repeat for each session on the list that catches your fancy, but be aware that there are only ten available slots.

Only registered DrupalCamp Charlotte attendees may vote, but anyone can register for the camp and registration is free. Voting will run until 5:00 PM on May 31st with the finalized session schedule will being posted on Friday, June 1st. Be sure to follow us on Twitter for frequent updates and help us get the word out.
